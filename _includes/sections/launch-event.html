Sprinting towards February 2019

In February 2019, we have our first milestone. It includes having published the time schedules by the 4 public transport agencencies as live as possible as Linked Connections, as well as having published a live Open Street Map version in routable tiles. These datasets will allow route planners to automatically adopt them, without having to go through expensive pre-processing steps, or manual integration labour.

A press moment at the Open Belgium 2019 conference will announce how we will organize a Call for Ideas and Call for Proposals for new route planners. The selected ideas will be built by students during Open Summer of code 2019!

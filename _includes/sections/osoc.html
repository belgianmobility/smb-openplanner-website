Open Summer of Code 2019

The proof of the pudding is in the eating. We will put our open source tools for data integration to the test during Open Summer of Code. 10 students will develop 3 concrete suggestions from the open call for ideas!

Already excited? Register at the Demo Day!

Check out the three selected projects

01. Configurator for individual route planners

Would you prefer to avoid mud? Do you dislike left turns? Do you wish to go downhill? This configurator will be created for anyone who is interested in configuring their very own route planner to answer your personal mobility needs. We will demonstrate our configurator using the velomobile as a test case .

Details after the begining of Osoc, see you on 04/07
02.Mobility scores dataset generator

Imagine real estate agents being able to better identify the value of a property by the score of its location and accessibility. Imagine two people being able to have a meeting at their most reachable mid-point. The mobility scores dataset generator allows developers to create a dating app, a real estate mobility app, a meeting planner, etc. using the mobility scores we provide.

Details after the begining of Osoc, see you on 04/07
03.E-Bike and Public Transport route planner

With an e-bike, it is easy to cover greater distances. This application will provide an intermodal route planner that demonstrates cyclists all different mobility options to use their e-bike in the best way.
